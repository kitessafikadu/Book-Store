Welcome to Feedmind, a Django-based book store. Our team consists of fourth-year Software Engineering students from Jimma University, Ethiopia. We are driven by a passion for creating innovative solutions that address pressing community challenges. Through technology, we aim to bridge gaps and bring about impactful change.

Feedmind was born from our desire to address the issue of limited access to books and knowledge resources. Our platform connects individuals who have home-based personal libraries, transforming them into a global network of shared knowledge. Users can upload books they want to share while also exploring and accessing books shared by others around the world.

As a team, we are committed to tackling real-world problems with cutting-edge solutions. Our work is fueled by a shared vision to make knowledge more accessible and inclusive for everyone, fostering collaboration and growth within communities.

Together, let's make a difference!
